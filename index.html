We’re a Partnership of Clinical Psychologists

We have over 10 years of experience practicing in the United States, and in Hong Kong. Services are offered in individual as well as group settings. Sessions can be held in either English, Cantonese, or Mandarin.

Positive Psychology 
Guides Our Practice

Positive psychology is a burgeoning movement that emphasizes positive human qualities, diverting mainstream psychology’s preoccupation with human weaknesses. It’s our core belief and guides the way we help our clients.

We Help You Take Better Care of Children

Our expertise is especially helpful for caretakers, child-care workers, and teachers as well as parents. We’re also trained and experienced in working with adults, treating issues pertaining to women, and the elderly.

New Book Published !

Dr. Winnie Keung published her first book earlier this year! Titled【孩子扭計點算好】 – it’s a short read about common behavioural situations faced by parents and care-givers of children aged 4 to 13.

Written in a simple case-study format, Winnie provides a balanced perspective of child-developmental psychology, offering techniques and advice for effective parenting, preventing emotional distresses, and promoting a harmonious domestic environment for mental growth and development.

New Service !

We are now certified Cogmed coaches!

Cogmed Working Memory Training is a computer-based solution for attention problems caused by poor working memory, combining cognitive neuroscience with games to challenge and improve your working memory capacity.

We help you train a tightly defined cognitive function to create a cascade of improvements that generalizes to behaviour, improving your ability to pay attention, resist distractions, self-manage, and learn.
