HOME Psychological Services provides assessments, counseling, and intervention across a range of psychological disciplines. We have over 10 years of clinical experience working with children, adolescents, and families.

Our practice is guided by the tenets of positive psychology – seeking fulfillment in normal life by focusing on positive human qualities, diverting mainstream psychology’s preoccupation with human weaknesses. Consultations and sessions can be held individually or in a group setting. Our practice is conducted in English, Cantonese, or Mandarin. We are also experienced in assessing and treating more specialized issues in adults with a focus on females, the elderly, and married couples.
